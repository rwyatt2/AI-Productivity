## Focus

* **SLOs & freshness:** Latency, freshness, and reliability expectations for data.
* **Stakeholders:** Who consumes the data; what decisions depend on it.

Keep scope and acceptance criteria aligned with data deliverables (datasets, dashboards, pipelines), not application code.
